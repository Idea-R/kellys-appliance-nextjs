## NOTES

- **GBP posting is the #1 highest-ROI activity** for local pack visibility. Two posts per week minimum.
- **Reviews drive AI search citations.** AI now summarizes review sentiment when recommending businesses.
- **Search Console revalidations** take 1-4 weeks. Don't re-request until the current validation completes.
- **Blog content should answer questions conversationally** — this is how AI search picks up your content.
