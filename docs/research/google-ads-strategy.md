## AI FEATURES: ENABLE vs. AVOID

### Enable
- [x] AI Max for Search — 14-27% conversion lift proven
- [ ] Text Guidelines — exclude "cheap/bargain/discount", set brand voice
- [ ] Smart Bidding (Maximize Conversions initially, Target CPA after 30+ monthly conversions)
- [ ] Responsive Search Ads — provide many headline/description options
- [ ] Call assets on every campaign

### Use with Caution
- [ ] Performance Max auto-generated creatives (upload your own videos instead)
- [ ] Broad match without AI Max guardrails

### Avoid
- Do NOT auto-apply all Google recommendations
- Do NOT use "Presence or Interest" location targeting (use "Presence" only)
- Do NOT skip negative keywords on any campaign
- Do NOT spread budget across too many campaigns initially
