## Campaign Notes

### Why One Campaign, Multiple Ad Groups
- $20/day budget is pooled across all ad groups
- Google's Maximize Conversions bidding optimizes across ad groups automatically
- Separating into multiple campaigns would split the budget and reduce optimization
- Each ad group can have its own tailored ad copy while sharing the budget
- If we increase budget later, we can split into separate campaigns for more control

### Ad Copy Strategy Per Ad Group
- **Main:** Generic appliance repair + local trust signals
- **Symptoms:** "Having this problem? We can fix it" approach
- **Competitors:** Position Kelly's as the better alternative (never mention competitor names)
- **Brands:** Highlight factory-authorized status for applicable brands
- **Misspellings:** Same as Main ad copy (Google shows the ad regardless of the typo)
- **Commercial:** Emphasize commercial/restaurant experience

### Budget Scaling Plan
- Start at $20/day with Main ad group only
- After 2 weeks of data, add Symptoms and Brands ad groups
- After 1 month, evaluate and add Competitors if budget allows
- Consider increasing to $30-40/day after proving ROI
- Commercial ad group adds when ready to actively pursue restaurant/commercial clients

### Key Metrics to Track
- Cost per conversion (target: under $50)
- Click-through rate by ad group
- Quality Score per keyword
- Conversion rate by ad group
- Impression share (are we showing for all searches?)
- Geographic performance (which cities convert best?)
