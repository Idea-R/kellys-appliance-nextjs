# Nextdoor Ads — Research Report for Kelly's Appliance Center

**Date:** April 7, 2026

## Overview

Nextdoor is a neighborhood-based social platform where verified residents discuss local topics, recommend businesses, and share community updates. 77% of users are homeowners. 2.4x more trusted than competitor platforms for local recommendations.

## How It Works

### Free Tier (Business Page)
- Claim/create business page
- 2 free posts per month to all neighbors within 2 miles
- Estimated reach: 5,000-20,000 homeowners per post in suburban areas
- Collect "Faves" and recommendations
- Need at least 1 recommendation to be visible in search
- Cost: $0

### Paid Tier (Nextdoor Ads Manager)
- Starting budget: $3-5/day
- Cost: ~$20 CPM (cost per 1,000 impressions)
- Placement: Newsfeed and For Sale & Free section
- Billing: Daily budget x 31 days, charged upfront, recurring

### Targeting Options
- Location: state, city, zip code, neighborhood radius
- Demographics: age, gender, household income
- Homeownership status
- Interests and behavior on Nextdoor
- Custom audiences (retarget/exclude)
- Weather targeting (temperature-triggered)
- Device targeting

### AI Features (2025-2026)
- Click optimization: 134% CTR lift vs CPM bidding
- Conversion optimization: 35% improvement in CPA

## Fit for Kelly's Appliance

### Strong fit because:
- 77% homeowners = exact customer demographic
- Hyper-local targeting = Sonoma/Marin/Napa focus
- Word-of-mouth is Kelly's primary growth channel — Nextdoor digitizes this
- Appliance repair is highly recommended category on Nextdoor
- 76% of members influenced by neighbor suggestions
- Kelly's already gets organic traction on Nextdoor

### Concerns:
- Mixed ROI reviews from advertisers
- Pricing reportedly increased mid-2025
- Smaller audience than Google (passive browsing vs active search intent)
- Some users report ads not appearing despite being charged
- Cap of ~$300/month reported as sweet spot

## Recommended Approach

### Phase 1: Free Page (Immediate, $0)
1. Create/claim Kelly's Appliance business page
2. Get first recommendation from existing customer
3. Post 2x/month with seasonal tips, service announcements
4. Build Faves and organic recommendations

### Phase 2: Paid Test (Week 3-4, $5/day = $155/month)
- Sponsored post targeting homeowners within 15 miles of Cotati
- Simple message: "Your neighbor's appliance repair shop since 1975"
- Measure clicks, phone calls, form submissions
- Run for 2 weeks, evaluate ROI

### Phase 3: Scale or Cut (Month 2+)
- If working: increase to $10/day ($310/month)
- If not: keep free page active, cut paid ads
- Seasonal campaigns for holidays, summer

## Budget Recommendation
- Sweet spot: $150-300/month for paid ads
- Free page: always active
- Total Nextdoor investment: $0-300/month depending on results

## Nextdoor vs Google Ads

| Factor | Google Ads | Nextdoor |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Intent | High (active search) | Low-medium (browsing) |
| Cost | $3-6/click | ~$20 CPM |
| Audience | Anyone searching | Verified local homeowners |
| Trust | Ad = ad | Neighbor recommendation = gold |
| Best for | Immediate leads | Brand awareness + trust |
| ROI timeline | Days | Weeks/months |

## Bottom Line

Google Ads = lead engine (catches broken fridge today).
Nextdoor = trust engine (ensures neighbor recommends Kelly's tomorrow).
Both matter. Run both.
