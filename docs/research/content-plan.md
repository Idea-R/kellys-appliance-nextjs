### Photo Tips
- Use natural lighting when possible
- Horizontal orientation preferred for GBP
- Include people when possible (builds trust)
- Show branded uniforms/vehicles in every shot
- Minimum resolution: 720x720px, recommended 1200x900px
- Geo-tag photos to Cotati, CA for local SEO signal
- Add photos weekly (not all at once — signals ongoing activity)

### Review Timing Strategy
- Send text/SMS within **2 hours** of completed service (while experience is fresh)
- Send email follow-up **next morning** if no review yet
- **Never** incentivize reviews with discounts (violates Google TOS)
- Ask **every** customer, not just the happy ones
- Track review request → conversion rate monthly

## DIRECT GOOGLE REVIEW LINK

To generate:
1. Search "Kelly's Appliance Center" on Google
2. Click your GBP listing → "Write a review"
3. Copy that URL
4. Or use: `https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=[PLACE_ID]`
- [ ] Find and save the direct review link
- [ ] Create short URL (e.g., bit.ly/kellys-review)
- [ ] Generate QR code from the link
