## Strategy Assessment

YouTube is the **#2 search engine** and feeds Google AI Overviews. For appliance repair:

1. **"How to fix" videos** get massive search volume
2. **VideoObject schema** on embedded videos boosts SEO
3. **Brand trust** — showing real technicians builds credibility
4. **AEO play** — YouTube content is cited in AI answers

**Content ideas:**
- "Is Your [Appliance] Broken? 5 Signs to Watch For" (matches existing blog topics)
- "When to Repair vs Replace Your [Appliance]"
- Before/after repair walkthroughs
- "Meet the Kelly's Team" intro video
- Seasonal maintenance tips (60-second shorts)
