## What Is It

BrightLocal is a **local SEO tool** (not a marketing platform). It provides:
- Local search rank tracking across cities
- Google Business Profile audit
- Citation tracking (NAP consistency across directories)
- Review monitoring and generation
- Local SEO reporting

## Why Consider It

Currently we're manually pulling Search Console data for city-level performance. BrightLocal would automate:
1. **Rank tracking** — Track "appliance repair" + each city automatically
2. **Citation audit** — Find/fix inconsistent Name/Address/Phone across directories
3. **Review monitoring** — Aggregate reviews from Google, Yelp, Facebook in one dashboard
4. **Competitor tracking** — See where competitors rank vs Kelly's per city
5. **White-label reports** — Professional reports for Mark (owner)
