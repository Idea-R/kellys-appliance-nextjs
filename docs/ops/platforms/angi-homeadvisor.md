## Platform Assessment

Angi merged with HomeAdvisor. They sell leads to service providers at $15-80/lead depending on category. Appliance repair leads typically run $20-40 each.

**Pros:**
- High-intent leads (people actively searching for repair)
- Google Guaranteed-like trust signals
- Good for filling schedule gaps

**Cons:**
- Lead quality varies (shared leads sent to multiple providers)
- Can't control budget as tightly as Google Ads
- Monthly minimums and contracts
- Some providers report high cost-per-acquisition
- Competes with our own SEO and Google Ads
