## Three things that matter

1. **Google Ads Search is the proven winner.** 95 conversions at $6.02, up 79% from April, and the leads it produces match to real jobs at 78%. It now carries 69% of paid conversions on 39% of spend. Recommendation: shift ~$300/mo from LSA into Search.

2. **Santa Rosa is the biggest gap, in every channel.** Weakest organic ranking, most expensive paid keyword ($10.96 CPA). It's the largest city in the service area. A dedicated Santa Rosa content + landing-page push would lift paid and organic together.

3. **Two channels are invisible to us.** Thumbtack and Angi have produced **zero** leads ever — confirmed there's no API for either, so capturing them needs browser automation. And Yelp gave us 32 requests we can't measure because it hides the phone number. Both are likely real money/demand we're flying blind on.

> **Organic SEO update:** the May Search Console pull came in via browser at **639 clicks / 55.8K impressions / position 11.3** (rankings improving from 14.2). Biggest prize: "oven repair" shows **10,466 impressions and zero clicks** at position 22.9. Full detail in the SEO report.
