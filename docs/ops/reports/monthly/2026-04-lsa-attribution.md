## Notes on Data Hygiene

1. **Some leads weren't in the OATAS sd_jobs table** because they're brand new and the office hasn't logged them yet, OR because they didn't book.
2. **Phone format normalization works** — OATAS handles `+1XXXXXXXXXX`, `XXX-XXX-XXXX`, and 10-digit equally well.
3. **Multi-phone households** — some matches showed dual phones (e.g., `415-722-4873 707-774-5920`) suggesting the household has both numbers; either may match.
4. **Sync staleness** — OATAS sync_status was RED (424 min stale) at pull time. A few late-April matches may have been missed if not yet synced.
