## Five Key Findings

1. **LSA is Kelly's single best-performing paid channel.** 131 leads in 30 days, 47% match-rate to real CRM jobs, estimated $8,400+ in revenue on $531 spend.

2. **Google Ads Search is proving itself profitably.** After launching April 6, the Search campaign has 8 conversions at a $15.58 CPA — lower than LSA. The $600 Google credit is unlocked and ready to redeem before the April 23 deadline.

3. **One keyword is carrying the Search campaign.** The broad phrase match "appliance repair" accounts for 28 of 44 clicks (64%) and 6 of 8 conversions (75%). Highly efficient, but over-concentrated.

4. **29 of 46 active keywords have zero clicks.** Many are impressions-only. Major optimization opportunity — pause dead weight, expand what's converting.

5. **Organic SEO is a sleeping giant.** The blog drives 253+ clicks to a single post and 121 quick-win opportunities sit unused across service location pages. This is free traffic waiting for optimization.

## Critical Issues Requiring Attention

- **4 LSA leads stalled in "Working to Schedule" for 5-8 weeks** — customers likely lost, ~$800-1,600 recoverable revenue
- **32 LSA leads charged but unmatched to CRM** — ~$3,200-6,400 potential missed revenue, root cause unknown without SCS call recording review
- **Zero LSA lead grading being submitted to Google** — hurting LSA ranking algorithm
- **Google Ads Search attribution is blind** — no way to tie Search conversions to actual CRM jobs yet (fix: 2.5 hrs of dev work)
- **Santa Rosa organic ranking stuck at position 8.1** — biggest SEO gap in the market
