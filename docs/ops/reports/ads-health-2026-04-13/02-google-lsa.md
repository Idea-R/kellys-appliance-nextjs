## Unmatched Leads (32 Charged Leads with No CRM Record)

32 LSA leads in 2026 were charged by Google but don't appear in the ServiceDesk CRM. Possible explanations:

1. **Inquiry only (no book)** — Customer called, got a quote, decided not to proceed or went with a competitor
2. **Missed call / voicemail** — Call not answered, no callback made
3. **Out of service area** — Call received, customer was outside Kelly's coverage
4. **Spam / wrong service** — Robocall or someone looking for a service Kelly's doesn't provide
5. **Sale call / vendor** — Not a customer, someone trying to sell Kelly's something

**Estimated missed revenue range:** Of the 32, if even half were real customers who didn't book, that's 16 potential jobs × ~$300 average = **$4,800 in potential missed revenue**.

**Action:** Listen to SCS call recordings for each of these numbers, categorize outcome, grade the lead accordingly in the LSA dashboard.

## Lead Management Gap

As of April 13:
- **45 of 50 recent leads are sitting at "Active" status** (not marked BOOKED, not graded)
- **Messages going unread for days** (one from April 12 still unread)
- **Zero lead grading feedback submitted to Google**

**Why this matters:** Google's LSA algorithm uses engagement data to improve lead targeting for your business. When you mark leads as BOOKED or grade them with feedback, Google learns what customer profiles are valuable to you. When leads sit unmanaged, Google has no signal — meaning the algorithm can't improve targeting, and your ranking in the LSA auction suffers.

## LSA Validation SOP
