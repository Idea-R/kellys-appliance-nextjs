## Where this stands

Kelly's has two locations in Google Business Profile Manager:
- **Cotati** — 466 Primero Court, Suite H, Cotati, CA 94931 → **Verified** (the good, live profile).
- **Santa Rosa** — 366 Brockhurst Drive, Santa Rosa, CA 95401 (store code `01968381162693272007`) → **Duplicate** (not public, blocked).

**Why "Duplicate":** Kelly's is a service-area business, and Google only allows one profile for the area a service business covers. Adding a second profile with the same name for a city already served gets auto-flagged as a duplicate of the Cotati profile. A Maps check of 366 Brockhurst confirmed there is **no conflicting third-party listing** at that address, so this is purely the same-business policy match, not a listing to claim or merge. The Santa Rosa market is held by competitors (TeeVax, Matt's Wholesale, Gold Ridge Appliance Repair, Sears), which is exactly why a presence there matters.

**The case for the second location:** it is a real, staffed satellite office with its own signage, its own dedicated local phone line, and office space, staffed during business hours. That gives a legitimate basis to verify it as a distinct location. The path is **video verification** (and a support request if it stays stuck).

**Risk to keep in mind:** Google scrutinizes second locations for service businesses (it is a common spam pattern). Push it cleanly and accurately. Overclaiming a walk-in storefront is what gets listings suspended later, and suspensions can occasionally affect the account. Keep the framing accurate: a staffed satellite office.

**Santa Rosa dedicated line:** 707-585-0517 (local 707, distinct from the Cotati number — the single strongest "two real locations" signal).

## Office prep checklist (do before filming)

You already have the three things that matter: signage, a number, an office. This is the prep to make the video pass on the first try.

- [ ] **Signage is visible from outside** — the "Kelly's Appliance Center" name readable on the door, window, or building.
- [ ] **The street address number (366) is visible** on the building or unit, and not obstructed.
- [ ] **Office is presentable and looks operational** — desk, work area, any branded material (business cards, branded paperwork, a sign inside).
- [ ] **The 707-585-0517 line is physically reachable at this office** and you can demonstrate it ringing there (have a second phone ready to call it during the video).
- [ ] **Proof-of-operation document on hand** — a piece of business mail or a utility bill addressed to Kelly's Appliance Center at 366 Brockhurst Drive. This is the strongest single piece of evidence.
- [ ] **Hours posted or known** — be ready to state the staffed hours; they should match what you set on the listing.
- [ ] **Film in landscape, good lighting, steady hands, one continuous take** (no cuts, no edits — Google rejects edited video).

## Video verification script (single, unbroken take, ~60-90 sec)

Narrate as you go.

1. **Outside at the street:** "This is Kelly's Appliance Center at 366 Brockhurst Drive, Santa Rosa, California." Pan to show the building and the **signage** with the name visible.
2. **At the door:** show the **address number (366)** clearly.
3. **Walk inside, still recording:** show the **office space** — desk, work area, anything branded.
4. **Show the phone:** "Our dedicated line here is 707-585-0517." If possible, **call it from another phone and show it ringing** on the line at this location.
5. **Proof you operate here:** show a piece of **business mail or a utility bill** addressed to Kelly's at 366 Brockhurst Drive.
6. **Close:** "This is our staffed Santa Rosa location." Stop recording.
