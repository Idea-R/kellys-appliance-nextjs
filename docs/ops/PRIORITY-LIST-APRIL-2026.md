### 2. CTR Improvement — Meta Title/Description Rewrites

These pages have high impressions but terrible CTR. The titles and descriptions aren't compelling people to click. This is the highest-ROI SEO work because we're already ranking — we just need people to choose us.

#### The Problem

Most location pages have the same formula:
- Title: `Appliance Repair [City] - Refrigerator, Oven, Washer & Dryer Service`
- Description: `Factory-authorized appliance repair in [City], CA. Serving [neighborhoods] since 1975...`

**This is generic and boring.** Everyone says "factory-authorized." Everyone lists appliance types. There's no reason to click Kelly's over the next result.

#### What Makes People Click

1. **Numbers** — "50+ Years", "90-Day Guarantee", "4.8 Stars"
2. **Social proof** — "Diamond Certified", "#1 Rated"
3. **Urgency/Availability** — "Same-Day Service", "Book Online Now"
4. **Differentiation** — What makes Kelly's different from the 10 other results?
5. **Emotional triggers** — Trust, reliability, family-owned
