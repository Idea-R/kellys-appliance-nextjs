### Weekly Content Distribution Plan

Each blog post generates 4-5 pieces of derivative content across platforms:

```
SUNDAY    Blog Post #1 published on website
          → GBP update (summary + link)
          → Facebook post (conversational intro + link)

MONDAY    → Instagram carousel (3-5 key tips from Sunday's blog)
          → LinkedIn post (professional angle of the same topic)

TUESDAY   → X thread (3-4 tweets: quick tips from blog)
          → GBP photo post (relevant appliance photo + tip)

WEDNESDAY Blog Post #2 published on website
          → GBP update (summary + link)
          → Facebook post (different angle than Sunday's)

THURSDAY  → Instagram post or reel (tips from Wednesday's blog)
          → LinkedIn post (if B2B relevant, otherwise skip)

FRIDAY    → X engagement (reply to appliance questions, local threads)
          → Facebook community post (not blog-related: local shout-out, team photo, fun fact)

SATURDAY  Rest / plan next week's content
```

### Platform-Specific Guidelines

#### Google Business Profile
- **Frequency:** 3-4x/week (every blog + 1-2 standalone tips/photos)
- **Format:** Update post with photo, 100-300 words, CTA button
- **Best content:** Before/after repairs, seasonal tips, new blog announcements, parts specials
- **Key rule:** Always include a photo. Posts with photos get 2x more engagement.

#### Facebook
- **Frequency:** 3x/week
- **Format:** Conversational post, 1-3 sentences + link or photo
- **Best times:** 9-11 AM or 1-3 PM, Wednesday/Thursday peak
- **Best content:** Blog recaps written as a conversation ("Hey Sonoma County, quick question..."), team photos, community shout-outs
- **Key rule:** Don't just share the link. Write a 2-sentence human intro.

#### Instagram
- **Frequency:** 2-3x/week
- **Format:** Carousel (tips), single photo (before/after, parts), Reels (60s repair tips)
- **Best times:** 9 AM Thursday, Wednesday overall best day
- **Best content:** Visual tips from blog posts as carousels, repair process photos, team content
- **Key rule:** Every post needs 3-5 relevant hashtags max (#appliancerepair #sonomacounty #kellysappliance #homerepair #[city])

#### LinkedIn
- **Frequency:** 2x/week
- **Format:** Text post (thought leadership), article shares
- **Best times:** Tue-Thu, 11 AM-5 PM
- **Best content:** Right to repair, industry insights, property manager outreach, "behind the business" stories
- **Key rule:** This is the B2B channel. Target property managers, realtors, contractors.

#### X (Twitter)
- **Frequency:** 3-4x/week
- **Format:** Quick tips (< 280 chars), threads (3-4 tweets), replies
- **Best content:** One-liner appliance tips, local Sonoma/Marin news replies, quick troubleshooting
- **Key rule:** Engage with others, don't just broadcast. Reply to local business threads.

### Content Recycling Framework

One blog post becomes 6+ pieces of content:

| Derivative | Platform | Format | Timing |
|-----------|----------|--------|--------|
| Blog post | Website | Full article (1,000-1,500 words) | Publish day |
| Summary + link | GBP | Update post with photo | Same day |
| Conversational recap | Facebook | 2-3 sentences + link | Same day |
| Key tips carousel | Instagram | 3-5 slide carousel | +1 day |
| Professional angle | LinkedIn | Text post, no link (algorithm prefers) | +1-2 days |
| Quick tip thread | X | 3-4 tweets | +2 days |
| Email snippet | Mailchimp | Include in weekly/monthly digest | End of month |

### What NOT to Do

- Don't post the same exact text on every platform (each platform has its own tone)
- Don't schedule everything robotically at :00 on the hour (feels automated)
- Don't post on weekends on LinkedIn (dead zone for B2B)
- Don't ignore comments/replies on any platform (engagement is the whole point)
- Don't use more than 5 hashtags on Instagram (looks spammy)
- Don't post without a photo on GBP (engagement drops 50%+)

## Image Strategy

### Image Sources (ranked by preference)

| Source | Type | Quality | Cost | Best For |
|--------|------|---------|------|----------|
| **Shane's own photos** | Real | Authentic | Free | Repair scenes, shop, vans, team, parts shelf |
| **AI-generated (image-gen skill)** | Realistic renders | High (4K) | Free | Hero images, appliance close-ups, conceptual |
| **Open image libraries** | Stock | Varies | Free | Generic appliance photos, backgrounds |
| **Cloudflare Image Optimization** | Delivery | N/A | Free (Pages) | All images run through CF for WebP/AVIF, lazy load |

### Open Image Libraries to Use

| Library | URL | License | Notes |
|---------|-----|---------|-------|
| Unsplash | unsplash.com | Free commercial | Good appliance/kitchen photos |
| Pexels | pexels.com | Free commercial | Similar to Unsplash |
| Pixabay | pixabay.com | Free commercial | Larger library, varies quality |
| StockSnap | stocksnap.io | CC0 | Smaller but curated |

### AI Image Generation Guidelines

- Use the `image-gen-prompt` skill for crafting prompts
- Target: photorealistic, 4K, kitchen/home setting, warm lighting
- Format for web: WebP, max 1200px wide, compressed for fast load
- Hero images: 1200x630px (also serves as OG image)
- In-post images: 800x600px or 16:9 ratio
- Always include alt text with keywords
- Store in `/public/images/blog/[slug]/`

### Image Workflow Per Post

1. **Hero image** (1200x630) — AI-generated or real photo. Also serves as OG/social share image.
2. **1-2 in-post images** — Close-ups of the appliance/part being discussed. Real photos preferred, AI as backup.
3. **Optimize** — Run through Cloudflare's auto-optimization or manually convert to WebP.
4. **Alt text** — Descriptive, includes target keyword naturally.

## Per-Post Checklist

Before publishing any blog post:

- [ ] Content drafted following `docs/BLOG-CONTENT-STRATEGY.md` voice rules

- [ ] Zero em dashes, zero AI-tell phrases
- [ ] Read aloud — sounds like a person
- [ ] H1 = title (only one), proper H2/H3 hierarchy
- [ ] FAQ section (3-5 questions) with FAQJsonLd schema
- [ ] BlogPosting JSON-LD schema
- [ ] At least 1 link to a service page

- [ ] `metadata` export: title, description, canonical, openGraph
- [ ] Post added to `src/data/blogPosts.ts`
- [ ] Hero image created (1200x630) + in-post images
