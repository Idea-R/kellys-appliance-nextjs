# Blog Content Strategy: Kelly's Appliance Center

## Editorial Voice Guidelines

All blog content must pass the humanize-writing skill (`.claude/skills/humanize-writing.md`) before publishing. The key rules:

- **No em dashes.** Use periods, commas, or colons instead.
- **No AI-tell phrases**: "delve," "game-changer," "leverage," "utilize," "robust," "cutting-edge," "in today's rapidly evolving landscape," "let's dive in," "without further ado," "it's worth noting," "a testament to," "cornerstone," "elevate," "streamline," "harness the power."
- **Use contractions**: "we're," "don't," "it's," "you'll," "we've."
- **Vary sentence length.** Mix short punchy sentences with longer explanatory ones.
- **Write like you're talking to a neighbor.** Kelly's is a family business in Sonoma County. The tone should be friendly, practical, and knowledgeable without being condescending.
- **Be specific.** Name the part, name the brand, name the county. Generic advice doesn't rank or convert.
- **Read it out loud** before publishing. If it sounds like a brochure, rewrite it.

### Brand Voice Quick Reference

| Trait | Do This | Not This |
|-------|---------|----------|
| Tone | Friendly, practical | Corporate, salesy |
| Pronouns | "We," "our technicians," "you" | "One should," "the customer" |
| Technical depth | Enough to be helpful | So much it overwhelms |
| Local flavor | Name the county, the street, the PG&E bill | Generic "Bay Area" fluff |
| CTA | Clear and helpful | Pushy or repetitive |

## SEO/AEO Guidelines

### Question-Format Titles
Voice search and Google's "People Also Ask" boxes favor question-based titles. At least 50% of blog posts should use question format:
- "Why Is My Dryer Taking So Long to Dry Clothes?"
- "How Long Do Major Appliances Last?"
- "What Does the Error Code on My Washer Mean?"

### FAQ Sections
Every blog post should include a 3-5 question FAQ section at the bottom using the `FAQJsonLd` component for rich snippets. Questions should mirror real "People Also Ask" queries from Google.

### Heading Hierarchy
- H1: One per page (the blog title)
- H2: Major sections
- H3: Subsections within H2
- Never skip levels (no H1 then H3)

### Internal Linking Strategy
- Every post links to at least 1 service page
- Every post links to at least 1 other blog post
- Use descriptive anchor text, not "click here"
- New posts should be added to the `blogPosts` array in `src/data/blogPosts.ts`

### Canonical URLs
- Every blog post needs `alternates.canonical` in its metadata export
- Format: `/blog/[slug]`
